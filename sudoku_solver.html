The Sudoku Solver

Instructions

Input your sudoku into the grid below and press the 'Solve' button. If you wish to clear the board, press the 'Clear' button.

The alogirthm behind this page uses two methods: elimination, where cells with only one possibility are filled in, and brute-force, where the possibilities of all cells are tried in turn. The algorithm will first try to use the elimination method. If it works, your sudoku will be solved almost instantaneosuly. However, if it fails, it will resort to the brute-force method. You will know that this has happened when the page appears to freeze after clicking the 'Solve' Button.

At this stage, please wait for around 6 to 10 minutes for the brute-force algorithm to solve the board. Bear in mind that this will take place a heavy load on your CPU. If you wish to stop the brute-force algorithm before it has finished for whatever reason, please force-shut Chrome via the Task Manager. It is recommended to not have other tabs or programs running while the algorithm is working.
